# Luna Loops Onboarding Copy

Design document for the interactive onboarding tutorial flow.

---

## Philosophy

**Voice rules (non-negotiable):**
- Spare, grounded, warm. Never twee, never grandiose.
- No "journey", "manifest", "embrace", "beautiful", or "the universe has plans"
- Ground abstractions in sensory detail
- Match energy to phase (waxing = building, full = peak/clarity, waning = releasing, new = stillness/seeds)
- Not therapeutic. Not congratulatory. Honest.
- Teaching through invitation, not instruction
- Descriptive, not prescriptive

## 2. Tab Tours

### 2.1 Sky Tab Tour (4 steps)

**Step 1 — Moon Display**
> **This is now.**
> The moon as it appears tonight, wherever you are. Tap it to go deeper into what this phase is asking.

**Step 2 — Phase Name & Guidance**
> **The phase has a quality.**
> Each of the eight phases carries a different kind of energy — building, questioning, releasing. The text below the moon describes what is live right now.

**Step 3 — Zodiac Transit**
> **The moon moves through the zodiac too.**
> Every few days, it shifts signs. This adds another layer — not rules, just texture. A watery sign feels different from a fiery one.

**Step 4 — Phase Timeline**
> **Eight stations.**
> This row shows where you are in the cycle. The current phase is lit. Tap any phase to see what that part of the cycle holds.

---

### 2.2 Loops Tab Tour (4 steps)

**Step 1 — What is a loop?**
> **Loops are intentions with a timeline.**
> Some span a single lunar phase (~3 days). Others follow the whole cycle (~29 days). All of them close when the moon does.

**Step 2 — Cycle Loops**
> **A cycle loop is a seed.**
> Set it at the New Moon. It holds your intention for the entire cycle — something to carry from dark to light and back to dark.

**Step 3 — Phase Loops**
> **A phase loop is a container.**
> Smaller, more immediate. What wants your attention during this specific phase? Steps can live inside it.

**Step 4 — Continue & Release**
> **Loops can continue or close.**
> When a cycle ends, you can release the loop completely or continue its intention into the next cycle. The choice is part of the practice.

---

### 2.3 Echoes Tab Tour (3 steps)

**Step 1 — What is an echo?**
> **An echo is a reflection.**
> Write or speak. Tag if you want. Each echo is anchored to the phase when you made it — a breadcrumb through the cycle.

**Step 2 — Voice Recording**
> **Your voice carries more than words.**
> The orb at the bottom records your voice. Speak what is true right now. It will be transcribed and saved.

**Step 3 — Tags & Filtering**
> **Find what you left.**
> Tags help you trace themes — grief, clarity, intention, body. Use the filters to see only echoes from specific phases or with certain tags.

---

### 2.4 Rhythm Tab Tour (5 steps)

**Step 1 — What is a rhythm?**
> **A rhythm is a named practice.**
> Not a habit to track. Not a goal to hit. A practice you want to observe as it moves through the phases.

**Step 2 — Setting Intention**
> **Intention is not a promise.**
> At the start of each cycle, you can set an intention for how you want to engage with this practice. No numbers. No pressure.

**Step 3 — Observation Dots**
> **The dots are not scores.**
> Each phase, you note what happened — present, partial, absent. The dots are observations, not judgments.

**Step 4 — The PhaseRing**
> **Two rings. Two truths.**
> The outer ring shows your intention. The inner ring shows what actually happened. The gap between them is not failure — it is information.

**Step 5 — Cycle Reports**
> **At the end, a report.**
> When the Waning Crescent arrives, you can see how the whole cycle unfolded. Not to grade yourself — to notice patterns.

### 4.2 Key Concepts List

**For reference/glossary:**

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Phase** | One of eight lunar stations: New, Waxing Crescent, First Quarter, Waxing Gibbous, Full, Waning Gibbous, Last Quarter, Waning Crescent |
| **Cycle** | One complete lunation, ~29.5 days from New Moon to New Moon |
| **Waxing** | The building half — from New Moon to Full Moon |
| **Waning** | The releasing half — from Full Moon to New Moon |
| **Threshold phase** | Pivotal moments: New, First Quarter, Full, Last Quarter |
| **Flow phase** | Sustained energy: Waxing Crescent, Waxing Gibbous, Waning Gibbous, Waning Crescent |
| **Tide** | Position within a phase: opening, flowing, completing, closing |
| **Loop** | An intention with a lunar timeline (cycle or phase) |
| **Echo** | A reflection — written or spoken — anchored to a phase |
| **Rhythm** | A named practice observed across the lunar cycle |
| **PhaseRing** | Visualization showing intention (outer) vs observation (inner) |

### 4.3 Common Questions

**Q: Why lunar instead of weekly or monthly?**
> The lunar cycle is older than any calendar. It moves in a rhythm of building and releasing that matches how energy actually moves — not arbitrary boxes, but organic arcs.

**Q: Do I have to believe in astrology?**
> No. You can use this as pure structure without any metaphysical claims. The phases are real — they are the literal shape of reflected light. What you make of them is up to you.

**Q: What if I miss a phase?**
> Nothing breaks. The moon keeps moving. Gaps are part of practice too. Come back when you come back.

**Q: How is this different from habit tracking?**
> Habit trackers count. Luna Loops notices. There is no streak to protect, no score to optimize. Just observation across time.

**Q: Why eight phases?**
> The eight phases capture the full arc of growth and release. Four threshold moments (New, Quarter, Full, Quarter) and four flowing periods between them. Each has a different quality.

## Implementation Notes

### Component Integration Points

1. **Welcome Modal** → New component, triggered on first app launch
2. **Tab Tours** → Use a tooltip/spotlight library (e.g., react-joyride)
3. **Phase Ceremony** → Already exists as `NewMoonRitual` component, extend for Waning Crescent
4. **Help Tooltips** → Add `?` button to each tab header
5. **Key Concepts** → Could be a modal accessible from settings or help menu
6. **Empty States** → Replace current placeholder text in each tab

### Accessibility

- All tooltip content should be announced to screen readers
- Ensure keyboard navigation through tutorial steps
- Provide skip option for users who don't need onboarding
- Store onboarding completion state per-user

### Localization Readiness

- All copy in this document should be extracted to a translation file
- Keep sentences short and concrete for easier translation
- Avoid idioms that don't translate well
